Tacos

Description

Tacos are a mexican dish with a tortilla and meat, along with onions and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Taco meat
  • Taco shells
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Take your meat and season it after browning in pan
  2. Add meat to taco sheels
  3. Add toppings and other stuff
  4. Eat tacos
