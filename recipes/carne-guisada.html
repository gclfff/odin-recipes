Carne Guisada Recipe

Description

Carne Guisada is a mexican dish from blank that translates to "stewed meat." This dish is my favorite of any food

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Stew
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Take your beef and season it

  3. Add broth and other stuff
  4. Let it cook
